Lasagna

Description

This classic lasagna recipe is a hearty and delicious dish filled with layers of pasta, meat sauce, and cheese.

Ingredients

  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 2 cups marinara sauce
  • 2 cups mozzarella cheese, shredded
  • 1 cup ricotta cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 1/4 cup Parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Boil lasagna noodles and set aside.
  3. Cook ground beef, then mix with marinara sauce.
  4. In a bowl, mix ricotta cheese, egg, and Parmesan cheese.
  5. Layer the ingredients: noodles, meat sauce, ricotta mixture, and mozzarella cheese.
  6. Repeat layers, ending with mozzarella cheese on top.
  7. Bake for 30-40 minutes until golden and bubbly.
