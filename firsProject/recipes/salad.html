Salad

Description

This fresh and healthy salad is filled with vibrant vegetables and topped with a tangy vinaigrette. It's perfect as a side dish or a light meal.

Ingredients

  • 4 cups mixed greens (spinach, arugula, lettuce)
  • 1 cucumber, sliced
  • 1 tomato, chopped
  • 1/4 red onion, thinly sliced
  • 1/4 cup feta cheese, crumbled
  • 1/4 cup black olives
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon balsamic vinegar
  • Salt and pepper, to taste

Steps

  1. In a large bowl, combine the mixed greens, cucumber, tomato, red onion, feta cheese, and black olives.
  2. In a small bowl, whisk together the olive oil, balsamic vinegar, salt, and pepper.
  3. Pour the dressing over the salad and toss to combine.
  4. Serve immediately as a side dish or enjoy as a light, healthy meal.
