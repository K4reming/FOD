Pizza

Description

This homemade pizza is easy to make and can be customized with your favorite toppings. The crispy crust, savory sauce, and melted cheese make it irresistible.

Ingredients

  • 1 pizza dough
  • 1/2 cup tomato sauce
  • 2 cups mozzarella cheese, shredded
  • 1/2 cup pepperoni slices
  • 1/2 cup mushrooms, sliced
  • 1/4 cup olives, sliced
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 475째F (245째C).
  2. Roll out the pizza dough on a floured surface to your desired thickness.
  3. Transfer the dough to a pizza stone or baking sheet.
  4. Brush the dough with olive oil, then spread the tomato sauce evenly over the surface.
  5. Sprinkle mozzarella cheese on top of the sauce.
  6. Add pepperoni, mushrooms, olives, and any other desired toppings.
  7. Sprinkle oregano over the top.
  8. Bake for 12-15 minutes, until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbly.
  9. Let cool slightly, slice, and serve.
